Welcome to the bakery adoption center!

At the bakery adoption center, the scent of warm dough and a hint of clever mischief fill the air as freshly baked cat breads lounge around, meticulously making biscuits for their visitors. Choosing one is a careful process... some are golden and toasty, others soft and doughy!

Us kitties like to think of ourselves like little pastry asssitants, caring for younger kittens.


Our baker-adoption specialists help match each new parent with the perfect cat bread, ensuring their flavor is just right. Once selected, the loving loaf is wrapped up in their little basket, and ready to head home!

This is the story of how a cat bread like find's a home!
