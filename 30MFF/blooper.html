What would you do?

A film is never perfect on its first shot

Just like every film, life always has it's fair share of bloopers...


College isn’t meant to be perfect on the first try! Whether you’re a Fall freshman or a Spring senior, you will continue to learn more about yourself and make your way through difficult periods. Even though at times it can feel isolating or intimidating, give it time! You will find your community and create a space for yourself on campus. Much like in a film, things won’t always go according to plan, there will be some bloopers along the way. But just like in the best movies, those moments of imperfection are a part of your story. So keep filming! Even the mistakes are worth remembering; tthey’re what give your film its character.
